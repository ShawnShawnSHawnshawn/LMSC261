My Favorite Animal: Gibbon

My favorite animal is the gibbon.

Gibbons are amazing because they look a little dramatic, a little confused, and somehow still very cool.

They swing through the trees like they have somewhere extremely important to be.

Top 5 Things I Like About Gibbons

  1. Their arms are very long
  2. They move through trees in a very funny and impressive way
  3. They have loud and interesting voices
  4. They look both serious and silly at the same time
  5. They seem like tiny forest acrobats

3 Fun Facts About Gibbons

  • Gibbons are famous for swinging from branch to branch
  • They use very loud calls to communicate
  • They are smaller than great apes, but they are still very talented climbers
